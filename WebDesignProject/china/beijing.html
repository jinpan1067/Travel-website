Cultural trip in Beijing

explore this long and history city

5 Days / 4 Nights

Please call us for booking or asking questions: (514)-5661234

Day 1: Beijing Capital International Airport – Beijing
Arrive Beijing Airport, meet & assist by our representative who will assist you in the Visas formalities. Then your vehicle will drive you to your hotel in Beijing for overnight
Day 2: Tiananmen Square – Forbidden city – Beijing Hutong
Breakfast at the hotel. Drive you to Tiananmen Square. walking into Forbidden city, spent about 3hours inside.Then we will visit Beijing Hutong (In Beijing, hutongs are alleys formed by lines of siheyuan, traditional courtyard residences).
Day 3: Summer Palace– Beijing
Breakfast at the hotel. Drive you to Summer Palace , we will be back to hotel around 5pm.
Day 4: Great Wall - Beijing
Drive you to Great Wall in north of Beijing city. back around 6pm to hotel.
Day 5: Departure Beijing Capital International Airport
Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer from Hotel to Beijing Airport for departure
