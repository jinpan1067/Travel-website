let's start a cultural trip in Beijing

Beijing is the capital of People's Republic of China. it is the world's most populous national capital city. The city is renowned for its opulent palaces, temples, parks, gardens, tombs, walls and gates. It has seven UNESCO World Heritage Sites—the Forbidden City, Temple of Heaven, Summer Palace, Ming Tombs, Zhoukoudian, and parts of the Great Wall and the Grand Canal—all of which are popular tourist locations

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding, or simply Chengdu Panda Base, is a non-profit research and breeding facility for giant pandas and other rare animals. It is located in Chengdu, Sichuan, China. it's the only one place to see all ages of pandas. You may have chance to hold a panda baby here. You will never forget this wonderful adventure.

Guilin karst topography

Guilin is a city in North Guangxi, China. Guilin is a scenic town and one of the best-known tourist destinations in China.The city has long been renowned for its scenery of karst topography. Guilin is one of China's most popular tourist destinations, and the epithet "By water, by mountains, most lovely, Guilin" (山水甲天下) is often associated with the city.
